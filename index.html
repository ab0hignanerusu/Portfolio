Login and Registration

  1. Login: The process of accessing a system or platform by providing valid credentials such as username and password, granting authorized users entry to their accounts or services.
  2. Registration: The initial step where users provide personal information to create an account, typically including username, password, and sometimes additional details, allowing access to services or content.
  3. Purpose: Login ensures authenticated access for existing users, while registration establishes new user accounts, collectively managing user identity and security within digital platforms and systems.
